Hi, I'm Deeparghya Dutta Barua, a first-year PhD student at the School of Computing and Augmented Intelligence (SCAI) at Arizona State University, advised by Prof. Pooyan Fazli. My primary interests lie in multimodal comprehension, and natural language processing.

I completed my undergrad from the University of Dhaka with a degree in Computer Science and Engineering. In my free time, I enjoy building tools that solve very niche but practical problems.
